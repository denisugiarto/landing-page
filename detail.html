Description

Tomb Of The Mask neon is a arcade game, where the player has to go through a different maze levels, evade generated obstacles, and laberinths full of mortal traps and enemies. To control our little tomb, you need to slide the finger across the screen in the desired way until it collides with an obstacle. Thanks to the masks that open as you progress through the missions, you receive different super powers. Evade spikes, traps, and neon enemies in your way to reach the secret Tomb Mask
